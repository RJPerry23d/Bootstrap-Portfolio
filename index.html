About Me

Hello my name is RJ Perry. I live in Raleigh, NC. I graduated the university of North Carolina at Pembroke with a BA in History. I moved to Raleigh in 2000 and landed a job in the tech industry. I have always been interested in IT. It started with me in the 4th grade.

I have been building simple web pages for over 15 years. I decided to take my skills to the next level and really submerse myself in the technology. That is why I have started my trainng in full stack web development. With full stack web development I will get a grasp of not only frontend skills and concepts but I will also learn the backend, server side skills as well. This journey will lead me to be a more productive and valuable member of the IT industry.
